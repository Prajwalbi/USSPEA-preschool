Learn More About Our Work And Our Cultural Activities

At our preschool, we help children grow through fun and interactive learning. Our caring teachers use hands-on activities to encourage curiosity and creativity, making learning enjoyable for every child.

Vidya Sampoorna Student Progressive Education Academy (VSSPEA) offers your child an international playschool environment from where she or he can proceed to any type of mainstream school. We cater to children from 1.6 to 6 years of age. Children of the ages between 18 months and 6 years are extremely receptive. VSSPEA preschool has fun-filled and distinctive programs that make us the most sought after preschool by encouraging the children to understand and develop basic language/communication skills while also developing social skills, gross and fine motor skills and creativity at the same time, through songs, music, storytelling, puppet theatres and imaginative plays.

At Vidya Sampoorna Student Progressive Education Academy (VSSPEA) preschool, we strongly believe that each child is a unique person and imbibes social, physical and intellectual behavior over a period of time. VSSPEA preschool is an excellent platform to bring out the best in your child. We strongly believe that early learning helps the best growth of brain and body in a child and we are devoted and enthusiastic to groom the kids of tomorrow. We offer a full range of nursery, Montessori, activity-based teaching with advanced pedagogy.

We encourage young children to explore their environment and express themselves using multiple mediums, which may include speaking, singing, movement, drawing, painting, writing, building and designing, collage, dramatic play and music. The children have the opportunity to engage with in-depth studies of concepts, ideas, and interests, which arise within the group. Both large and small group teamwork is considered valuable and necessary to advance cognitive development. Great attention is given to the look and feel of the classroom. The environment is considered the "third teacher". Learning is a lifelong process; at VSSPEA preschool we ensure the right foundation!

Music, festivals, and cultural events are integral parts of our curriculum. These events teach children about diversity, respect, and working together, helping them become kind and open-minded individuals.
