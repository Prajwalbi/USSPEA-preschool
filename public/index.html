At our preschool, we help children grow through fun and interactive learning. Our caring teachers use hands-on activities to encourage curiosity and creativity, making learning enjoyable for every child.

Vidya Sampoorna Student Progressive Education Academy (VSSPEA) offers your child an international playschool environment from where she or he can proceed to any type of mainstream school. We cater to children from 1.6 to 6 years of age. Children of the ages between 18 months and 6 years are extremely receptive. VSSPEA preschool has fun-filled and distinctive programs that make us the most sought after preschool by encouraging the children to understand and develop basic language/communication skills while also developing social skills, gross and fine motor skills and creativity at the same time, through songs, music, storytelling, puppet theatres and imaginative plays.
