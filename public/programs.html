Our Unique Programs

VSSPEA Preschool has fun-filled and distinctive programs that encourage children to understand and develop basic language/communication skills, social skills, gross and fine motor skills, and creativity. Through songs, music, storytelling, puppet theatres, and imaginative plays, we ensure holistic development for every child.

Language and Communication Skills

Our programs enhance children's language and communication skills through interactive activities such as:

  • Storytelling: Engaging children with captivating stories to boost their vocabulary and comprehension.
  • Group Discussions: Encouraging children to share their thoughts and ideas, promoting effective communication.

Social Skills Development

We focus on developing children's social skills through various group activities and interactive play sessions:

  • Team Projects: Working together on projects helps children learn cooperation and teamwork.
  • Role-Playing: Imaginative play scenarios that teach empathy and social interaction.
